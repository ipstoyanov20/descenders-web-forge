Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Noodlecake Studios Inc. collects, uses, and protects your information when you play Descenders and use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, customize your rider, or contact us for support. This may include your username, email address, gameplay preferences, and device information. We also automatically collect certain information about your device, including your IP address, device type, operating system, and gameplay statistics such as completion times, stunt scores, and progression data.

Additionally, we may collect information about your interactions with our game, including features used, levels completed, crashes experienced, and time spent playing. This helps us improve the game experience and identify technical issues that need addressing.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including personalizing your gaming experience, providing customer support, and developing new features. Your gameplay data helps us balance difficulty levels, improve our procedural generation algorithms, and create better physics simulations.

We may also use your information to communicate with you about game updates, new features, tournaments, and other Descenders-related news. You can opt out of promotional communications at any time through your account settings or by contacting us directly.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy. We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our game, conducting business, or serving our users, provided they agree to keep this information confidential.

We may also disclose your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety. Aggregated, non-personally identifiable information may be shared with partners for analytics and improvement purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption of sensitive data, secure server communications, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Gameplay statistics and progression data are retained to maintain your game experience across sessions and devices. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting our support team.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can modify most of your information through your account settings within the game. For additional requests or questions about your data, please contact our privacy team. If you're located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to object to certain processing activities.

Children's Privacy

Our game is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can delete such information from our records.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of Descenders after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, our data practices, or your rights regarding your personal information, please contact us at privacy@noodlecake.com or write to us at Noodlecake Studios Inc., Privacy Department, [Address]. We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.
