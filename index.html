Game Features

Discover what makes Descenders the ultimate mountain biking experience

Procedural Generation

Every ride is unique with our advanced procedural generation system. Experience endless variety in terrain, obstacles, and challenges that keep you coming back for more.

Physics-Based Gameplay

Feel every bump, jump, and crash with our realistic physics engine. Master the art of momentum, balance, and control as you navigate through challenging mountain terrain.

Team System

Choose your tribe and compete for reputation. Each team offers unique challenges and rewards, creating a dynamic progression system that adapts to your playstyle.

Bike Customization

Customize your ride with a variety of bikes, parts, and gear. Unlock new equipment as you progress and create the perfect setup for your riding style.

Multiplayer Action

Race against friends and players worldwide. Compete in daily challenges, leaderboards, and special events to prove you're the ultimate downhill rider.

Epic Stunts

Pull off incredible tricks and stunts to boost your reputation. Master backflips, tailwhips, and other jaw-dropping maneuvers while maintaining control at breakneck speeds.
