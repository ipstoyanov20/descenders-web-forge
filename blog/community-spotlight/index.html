Community Spotlight: Best User-Created Moments

The Descenders community never ceases to amaze us with their incredible skills, creativity, and passion for extreme mountain biking. This month, we're highlighting some of the most spectacular moments captured by our amazing riders.

Epic Wins and Spectacular Fails

From death-defying jumps to hilarious crashes, our community has shared some truly unforgettable moments. These clips showcase not just the skill of our players, but the pure joy and excitement that Descenders brings to riders around the world.

Most Creative Screenshots

Beyond the incredible stunts, our community has an eye for capturing breathtaking moments. From sunset rides to dramatic crashes frozen in time, these screenshots showcase the artistic side of mountain biking.

Photography Contest

Our monthly screenshot contest continues to produce amazing results. This month's theme was "Extreme Weather" and the submissions featuring storms, snow, and fog were absolutely stunning.

Community Challenges

Every month, we set new challenges for our community to tackle. This month's "No Crash Wednesday" challenge saw over 10,000 players attempt to complete entire runs without a single crash. Only 127 succeeded, but the attempts were hilarious!

Tips from the Community

Our experienced riders love sharing their knowledge. Here are some of the best tips that emerged from community discussions this month:

  • Pre-load your jumps by compressing before takeoff for maximum air time
  • Use the environment to your advantage - rocks and trees can help redirect your momentum
  • Practice rhythm sections in free ride mode before attempting them in challenges
  • Don't be afraid to take alternate routes - sometimes the scary path is actually easier
