About Descenders

Discover the story behind the ultimate extreme downhill mountain biking experience

Our Story

Descenders is the ultimate extreme downhill mountain biking game that combines procedurally generated worlds with realistic physics-based gameplay. Developed by RageSquid and published by No More Robots, this game offers an unparalleled biking experience that pushes the boundaries of what's possible in extreme sports gaming.

Our team set out to create a game that captures the raw excitement and danger of extreme downhill mountain biking. Using advanced procedural generation technology, we ensure that every run is unique, providing endless replayability and fresh challenges for riders of all skill levels.

The physics-based gameplay system responds to every input, making each jump, turn, and crash feel authentic and impactful. Players must master the art of momentum and balance to navigate treacherous terrain and perform death-defying stunts.
