About Descenders

The ultimate extreme downhill mountain biking experience

Our Story

Descenders was born from a passion for extreme sports and cutting-edge gaming technology. We wanted to create the most authentic downhill mountain biking experience possible, where every ride feels unique and every crash has consequences.

Our innovative procedural generation system ensures that no two rides are ever the same. Combined with realistic physics and stunning visuals, Descenders delivers an adrenaline-pumping experience that captures the thrill of extreme mountain biking.

From humble beginnings to becoming a global phenomenon, Descenders has evolved into a comprehensive mountain biking universe where riders can test their skills, customize their bikes, and compete with others worldwide.
