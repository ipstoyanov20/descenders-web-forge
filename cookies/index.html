Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how Noodlecake Studios Inc. uses cookies and similar technologies on the Descenders website to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized content.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit our website. These files contain information that helps websites remember your preferences, login status, and browsing behavior. Cookies enable websites to provide a more personalized and efficient user experience.

Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, and web beacons, which serve similar purposes to cookies but may store different types of information or function differently across browsing sessions.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation, access to secure areas, and remembering your cookie preferences. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Performance Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about page views, loading times, and user navigation patterns. This data helps us improve website performance and user experience.

Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and choices, such as language settings, region selection, and customized content. They help provide a more personalized experience tailored to your interests and previous interactions with our website.

Analytics Cookies: We use analytics cookies to gather statistics about website usage, popular content, and user behavior patterns. This information helps us understand what content resonates with our audience and how to improve our website and game offerings.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, remember your preferences, and provide relevant content about Descenders. Cookies help us understand which sections of our website are most popular, how long visitors spend on different pages, and what content generates the most interest.

Additionally, cookies enable us to display personalized game content, remember your newsletter subscription preferences, and maintain your session when navigating between pages. This ensures a smooth and consistent experience throughout your visit to our website.

Local Storage and Game Data

Our website may use local storage to save your preferences, such as cookie consent choices, theme settings, and language preferences. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless necessary for providing requested services.

When you interact with embedded game content or demos on our website, we may store game-related data locally to preserve your progress and settings across sessions. This data remains on your device and can be cleared through your browser settings.

Third-Party Cookies

Our website may include content from third-party services such as social media platforms, video providers, or analytics services. These third parties may set their own cookies on your device. We do not control these third-party cookies and recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these services for more information about their cookie practices.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, delete, and block cookies. You can also set your browser to notify you when cookies are being placed on your device. However, disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality and user experience of our website.

To manage cookies in popular browsers: Chrome users can access settings through Menu > Settings > Advanced > Privacy and Security > Cookies. Firefox users can find options under Menu > Options > Privacy & Security. Safari users can control cookies through Preferences > Privacy. Internet Explorer and Edge users can manage cookies through Internet Options > Privacy.

Cookie Consent and Updates

When you first visit our website, you may see a cookie consent banner that allows you to accept or customize your cookie preferences. Your choices are remembered for future visits. We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any significant changes through our website or other appropriate means.
