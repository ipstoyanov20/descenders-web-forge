Cookies Policy

How we use cookies and tracking technologies

Last Updated: December 2024

This Cookies Policy explains how Noodlecake Studios Inc ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies in our mobile game Descenders and on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or use a mobile application. They are widely used to make applications work more efficiently and to provide information to the application owners.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of Descenders and our website. They enable core features such as:

  • Game progress and save data
  • User authentication and account management
  • Security features and fraud prevention
  • Basic website functionality

Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how players interact with our game and website by collecting anonymous usage information:

  • Game performance metrics and crash reports
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Website traffic and user behavior analytics
  • A/B testing for game improvements

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your gaming experience by remembering your preferences and settings:

  • Game settings and preferences
  • Language and region preferences
  • Accessibility settings
  • Previously viewed content

Advertising and Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver personalized advertisements and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns:

  • Targeted advertising based on interests
  • Frequency capping for advertisements
  • Marketing campaign performance measurement
  • Cross-platform user identification

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies. These include:

Analytics Services

Google Analytics, Unity Analytics, and other services that help us understand game usage and improve player experience.

Advertising Networks

Google AdMob, Facebook Audience Network, and other advertising partners that serve relevant ads to players.

Social Media Platforms

Facebook, Twitter, and other platforms for social sharing and authentication features.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for several purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our game services
  • To improve game performance and user experience
  • To personalize content and advertisements
  • To analyze usage patterns and trends
  • To prevent fraud and enhance security
  • To facilitate social media integration
  • To measure the effectiveness of our marketing efforts

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

Mobile Device Settings

Most mobile devices allow you to control tracking through system settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Privacy > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

Browser Settings

When visiting our website, you can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies only
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

Opt-Out Links

You can opt out of certain advertising cookies through these industry initiatives:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance: www.aboutads.info/choices
  • Network Advertising Initiative: www.networkadvertising.org/choices
  • European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance: www.youronlinechoices.eu

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect your experience with Descenders and our website:

  • Some game features may not function properly
  • Your preferences and settings may not be saved
  • You may see less relevant advertisements
  • We may not be able to provide personalized experiences
  • Some website features may become unavailable

Data Retention

The retention period for cookie data varies depending on the type of cookie:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close the app or browser
  • Persistent cookies: Stored for a predetermined period (typically 30 days to 2 years)
  • Local storage: Persists until manually deleted or app is uninstalled

International Data Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers may process cookie data outside of your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
